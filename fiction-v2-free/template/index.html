Clients Opinion

The Literary Circle, NIT Durgapur is one of the very few non technical clubs in a college teeming with tech clubs and scientific student bodies.

Amongst all of this, the Literary Circle is a breath of fresh air, encouraging creativity and free thought. The Literary Circle, through events like NITMUN, Verve and Literaria helps students get out of their creative cocoon, engage in healthy debate and exchange ideas among themselves.

The Yearbook, presented to the batch passing out, is a condensation of the four years spent in this place that has become an emotion. It is a memoir for every final year who has spent four years in this glorious institution.

Welcome to the Circle!
