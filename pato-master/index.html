Our Story

Verve, the annual literary cum youth fest , in itself is an embodiment of Energy and Enthusiasm. Being the only cultural fest in this college, The action packed 3 days of Verve is bound to engross you with its riveting events , enthralling Star Talks, and needless to mention, The Prom Night!

We at The Literary Circle are very excited to announce our big reveal for Verve XV. A strong lady for whom challenges in life could not pin her down and only stacked up more strength in her. What’s more thrilling is that Deepika Padukone and Meghna Gulzar’s next mega collaboration ‘Chhapaak’ is based on her life where Deepika will be seen essaying her role. 3rd March - 10:00 AM - SAC

TREASURE HUNT

Mystery? It's in the air around you. The creaking door knows of the ambushes of the past and the whistling wind conveys messages of love. There is so much more than what you feel, there is so much more to it than what is visible. Your blood will run cold for the enigma requires you to find the lost, remember the forgotten and resurrect the dead.

26th February - 6:00 PM - LG 12 OLD ACAD

KRIXITY

Ever wondered what would it be like to doubt every step and yet feel unsafe in the most familiar of places! This time Verve brings to you, Krixity, a border of bloodshed and a blast of exuberance. It is your one chance to be "Captain America" and launch your path to victory. It's not a war of knowledge, it is a combat of strategy. Be present in groups of 4 preferably. Exciting coupons and prize money await !!!

CONCORDIUM

An exemplary blend of vocabulary and fantasy, Concordium is bound to take you for a roller coaster ride. With a wide array of word games to test your agility, composure, and the ability to deceive others (we actually mean that part), this captivating event lets you CREATE YOUR OWN SUPERHERO - the one who'll prove to be your Saviour at the end!
